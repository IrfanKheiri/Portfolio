TFAL

A MONEY LAUNDERING
INVESTIGATION

TFAL is an interactive game designed to educate beginners
about the intricacies of anti-money laundering (AML)
practices.

Players take on the role of an AML investigator tasked with
unraveling a complex money laundering scheme involving the
use of GOLD.
