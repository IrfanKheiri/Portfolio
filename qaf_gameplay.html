Harnessing Power: Health, Abilities & The Letters

Health & Marij

Your life force, Marij, is both your health and your resource. Regenerate it by collecting fragments from fallen foes, but spend it wisely to fuel powerful abilities and agile movements. A dangerous balance must be struck.

The Letters System

The core of your power lies in ancient Letters. Equip them into your grimoire to form words and phrases, unlocking and customizing your active abilities. Experimentation is key to discovering the most potent combinations.

Abilities: The Six Sigil Categories

Master six distinct categories of sigils, each tied to a different aspect of mystical power. Your chosen sigils, empowered by the Letters you equip, define your combat style.

The Ascent: Dungeons, Death & Progression

The Cycle of Ascent

Death is not the end, but a lesson. Each run through the procedurally generated dungeons of Mount Qaf offers new challenges, resources, and opportunities to grow stronger for the next attempt.

Dungeons & Bosses

Traverse four tiers of randomly generated platforms, facing minibosses and a final, formidable boss in each. No two climbs are ever the same.

Currencies & Resources

Gather various currencies to purchase temporary boons, unlock permanent upgrades, and craft powerful gear between your attempts.

Bones & Transfusions

Collect the Bones of fallen warriors. Consume them for a burst of power or equip them for lasting effects. Transfuse Souls to unlock powerful meta-progression.

The Jinn Kings' Favor

The seven planets are ruled by seven Jinn kings. At the start of each run, the heavens align, granting you boons based on which kings are dominant. Slot their cosmic sigils into major and minor slots to dramatically alter your abilities and gameplay for that ascent.
