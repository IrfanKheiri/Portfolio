About Me

Irfan Kheiri is a solutions architect and technology leader who operates at the intersection of education, simulation, and AI. As Chief Solutions Architect and CTO at SoulTek Studios, he leads the development of intelligent training environments that combine medical accuracy with interactive design—most notably SimNurse, an AI-powered clinical simulation platform built to enhance decision-making for healthcare professionals through immersive, scenario-based learning.

With a background spanning decades in media and technology, Irfan brings clarity to complexity. His work ranges from building ontology-driven frameworks for pediatric care to developing ECG waveform generators in Godot. Each solution reflects a balance of technical depth, user-centered design, and real-world application.

In addition to his leadership at SoulTek, Irfan teaches Game Design in the UX/UI stream at Indus Valley School, mentoring students in the thoughtful design of interactive systems. His interests also extend into applied AI across sectors—from hyperspectral imaging in mineral exploration to educational games that distill complex financial concepts into engaging, structured learning.

Across every initiative, Irfan combines strategic insight, hands-on execution, and a commitment to building tools that educate, empower, and endure.
